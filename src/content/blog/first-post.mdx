## Why Cats are Not Doctors

Despite their many talents, cats have not yet mastered medical science. Here's why:

1. They lack opposable thumbs
2. They sleep 16 hours a day
3. They're more interested in chasing laser pointers than studying anatomy

But that doesn't mean they can't make you feel better!
